KernelPath - Learn OS Kernels Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Overview

KernelPath is an educational app that teaches operating system kernel concepts through interactive lessons, simulations, and a built-in terminal simulator. All content is bundled within the app and no user data is collected or transmitted.

Data Collection

KernelPath does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected.
  • No account or login is required.
  • No cookies or identifiers are used.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores your learning progress locally on your device (such as completed lessons and quiz scores). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

KernelPath does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

KernelPath is an educational app suitable for all ages. It does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
