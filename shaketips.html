ShakeTips Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Overview

ShakeTips is a motivational app that displays random tips and quotes when you shake your device. All content is bundled within the app and no user data is collected or transmitted.

Data Collection

ShakeTips does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected.
  • No account or login is required.
  • No cookies or identifiers are used.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included.

Motion Sensors

ShakeTips uses your device's motion sensor (accelerometer) solely to detect shake gestures and display a new tip. Motion data is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

In-App Purchases

ShakeTips offers an optional in-app purchase to unlock additional tips. Purchases are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit. The app stores only a local flag on your device to remember your purchase status. No payment or financial information is accessible to the developer.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores minimal preferences locally on your device (such as your purchase status). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

ShakeTips does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

ShakeTips is suitable for all ages. It does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
