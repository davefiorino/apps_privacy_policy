SuperRes Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Overview

SuperRes is an image enhancement and colorization app. All image processing is performed entirely on your device using on-device machine learning models. Your images never leave your device.

Data Collection

SuperRes does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user content. Specifically:

  • No images are uploaded to any server.
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected.
  • No account or login is required.
  • No cookies or identifiers are used.
  • No third-party services receive any data from the app.

Photo Library Access

SuperRes requests access to your photo library solely to let you select images for processing and to save results. Photos are accessed only when you explicitly choose to import or save an image. No photos are read in the background or transmitted anywhere.

Third-Party Services

SuperRes does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

SuperRes does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
