MotivateMe Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Overview

MotivateMe is a productivity and motivation app that helps you manage daily activities, set goals, track your mood, and monitor your progress. All your data is stored locally on your device.

Data Collection

MotivateMe does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third party. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected.
  • No account or login is required.
  • No cookies or identifiers are used.
  • No advertising or third-party SDKs are included.

Photo Library Access

MotivateMe may request access to your photo library to let you attach images to history entries. Photos you select are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

Calendar Access

MotivateMe may request access to your calendar to add your scheduled activities as calendar events. This data stays on your device and is managed by the system calendar. The developer has no access to your calendar data.

Notifications

MotivateMe can send local notifications to remind you of activities, mood check-ins, and goal deadlines. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores information you enter (activities, goals, mood entries, history, and preferences) locally on your device. This data remains on your device and is never accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

MotivateMe does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

MotivateMe does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
