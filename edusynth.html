EduSynth Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Overview

EduSynth is an educational synthesizer app for macOS that lets you learn subtractive synthesis by playing a virtual Moog-style instrument. All processing happens locally on your device.

Data Collection

EduSynth does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the developer or any third party. Specifically:

  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected.
  • No account or login is required.
  • No cookies or identifiers are used.
  • No advertising or third-party SDKs are included.

Audio Access

EduSynth uses your device's audio output to produce synthesizer sound. The app may request microphone access as part of the Core Audio framework, but it does not record, store, or transmit any audio data.

MIDI Devices

EduSynth can connect to external MIDI controllers to receive musical input. MIDI data is processed locally in real time and is never stored, logged, or transmitted.

Data Stored on Your Device

EduSynth does not store any personal data. Synthesizer settings and preferences exist only in memory during the app session.

Third-Party Services

EduSynth does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

EduSynth does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
