AirplanesDB Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Overview

AirplanesDB is an aviation reference app that lets you explore aircraft specifications, photos, and airport information. All content is bundled within the app and no user data is collected or transmitted.

Data Collection

AirplanesDB does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No user data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or tracking data is collected.
  • No account or login is required.
  • No cookies or identifiers are used.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores minimal preferences locally on your device (such as theme settings and favorite aircraft/airports). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

AirplanesDB does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

AirplanesDB is an educational app suitable for all ages. It does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
